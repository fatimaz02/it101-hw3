Site Chosen

I reviewed https://examplebadsite.com and found the following issues that make it a poor web design example.

  1. Poor color contrast makes text unreadable.
  2. Navigation menus are inconsistent.
  3. Images take too long to load.
  4. Pop-ups interrupt reading.
  5. Not mobile-friendly.
  6. Broken internal links.
  7. Outdated content and layout.
  8. No alt text for images.
  9. Autoplaying videos.
  10. Overuse of flashy animations.

Takeaways

Good websites focus on accessibility, organization, and user experience. Following HTML and CSS best practices helps avoid these issues.
